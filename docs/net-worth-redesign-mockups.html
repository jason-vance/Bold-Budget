The five screens

Ordered by how often you'll touch them: add, check net worth, drill into an account, review spending, move money.

How this maps to your code. Account and SignedMoney are new domain types alongside Money / Transaction. Budget gains an accounts dictionary and an AccountFetcher/Saver pair mirroring the existing worker pattern — plus a Firestore accounts subcollection (remember to update the deployed rules, not the stale repo file). Transaction gains kind + accountId / fromAccountId / toAccountId; existing rows migrate as account-less, and save(transaction:) also nudges the affected account's stored balance. Your RecurringExpense debts graduate into liability accounts. Nothing about the current spending views has to change to ship v2.0.
